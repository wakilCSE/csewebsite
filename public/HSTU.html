Chairman Message

Message from the Chairman of department of CSE

Welcome to the department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). This department conducts all of its academic activities under the faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) which is the second oldest faculty of Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University, Dinajpur-5200, Bangladesh. Although this department started its academic activities from July 13, 2016, its history goes back to 2004.

 

During the establishment of the faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) in 2004, two departments named as Computer Science and Information Technology (CIT) and Computer Engineering (CEN) were formed in order to offer the major courses for Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (B.Sc. in CSE) degree. Later on in July 13, 2016 both of the departments were unified to as Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) in order to accelerate the advancement in the academic journey.

 

This department offers the major courses for B.Sc. (Engineering) in CSE degree. Besides, this department also conducts a few courses for the other two degrees (B.Sc. (Engineering) in ECE and B.Sc. (Engineering) in EEE) offered from the faculty of CSE. Outside of the faculty, Dept. of CSE offers courses in the following faculties in different degrees based on their curricula; Faculty of Agriculture, Faculty of Business Studies, Faculty of Engineering, Faculty of Fisheries, Faculty of Science and Faculty of Social Science and Humanities.

 

The department of CSE has started the research based M.Sc. in CSE program from the semester of July-December, 2019.

 

The department of CSE is run by a group of talented, young, energetic, student-friendly, enthusiastic and skilled faculties to spread the knowledge of Computer Science and Information Technology. This department is contributing in teaching, research and industries by producing quality graduates with high morals for global development.

 

There are two student organizations under this department, CSE Club and Programmers’ Arena, to promote the knowledge sharing environment for the students of B.Sc. (Engineering) in CSE.

 

I feel proud of being a part of this department. I wish all the success, prosperity and well-being of this department.

 

 

 

 

 

Professor Adiba Mahjabin Nitu

Chairman

 

 



Chairman, Department of Computer Science and Engineering.
